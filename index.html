How to Play Move Your Face

  • Wait for the green rectangle to settle on your face before pressing Start
  • Move your face to direct the green rectangle
  • Aim for blue and teal balls to score points
  • Avoid red and orange balls
  • Hit 3 red or orange balls and you lose
  • Level up by earning points
  • Earn a prize with every level!
